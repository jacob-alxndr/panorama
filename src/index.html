Workshops

Sculpting

2-week workshop

This class is for the beginner or advanced artist. You can learn to sculpt in figurative or abstract, terracottva or plasteline, creating large or small projects, with or without an armature. You can sculpt from your imagination, drawings, or photo.

Printmaking

4-week workshop

Expand your creative practice with the magic of screen printing. Artists of all levels and stripes can benefit from adding screen printing to their toolbox. Learn the basics of getting an image from sketchbook to screen to paper, as well as tips for incorporating screen printing into other media, like painting, book arts, and sculpture.

2D Drawing

8-week workshop

The workshop will be structured around different techniques, such as, perspectival drawings, the process of collage, and crafting, to produce the appearance of the three-dimension. At its core, this workshop is about the joy of drawing and visual expressions.
